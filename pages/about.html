About Us

Welcome to Flower Shop, your number one source for all things flowers. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of floral arrangements, with an emphasis on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by Nishka, Flower Shop has come a long way from its beginnings in a small home office. When Nishka first started out, her passion for eco-friendly and locally sourced flowers drove her to start her own business.

We now serve customers all over the city and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
